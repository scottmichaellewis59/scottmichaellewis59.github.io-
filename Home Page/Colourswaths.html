Colour Swaths

To generate a colour swath:

  1. Below, for each colour (Red, Green, and Blue), enter numbers for colour intensity (0 to 255 with 0 being no intensity and 255 being maximum intensity)
  2. Enter a number to increment the colour by
  3. Press the refresh button

Your colour values will be saved for next time you visit this page.

You can click on the text to change this page's background colour.
